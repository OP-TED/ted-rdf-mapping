TED-SWS artefacts documentation

The TED-SWS artefacts are mainly the transformation rules needed by the TED-SWS system. These transformation rules, written in RML, are mapping the XML structure of TED standard forms notices to RDF, and are organised in mapping suites.

Also, carefully selected sample data are available for testing the mapping correctness and completeness.

We foresee to map all standard forms and then eForms according to this plan, prioritising some form number over other ones. In order to be consistent in our approach, we also have established the mapping methodology.
